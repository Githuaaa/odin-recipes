Pasta Salad's Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked pasta (e.g., rotini, fusilli)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta: Cook 2 cups of pasta according to package instructions. Drain and let cool.
  2. Prep vegetables: While pasta is cooling, halve 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, dice 1 cup of cucumber and 1/2 cup of red bell pepper, and finely chop 1/2 cup of red onion.
  3. Combine ingredients: In a large bowl, mix the cooled pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, 1/2 cup of sliced black olives, and 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese.
  4. Add dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup of olive oil and 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar. Pour over the pasta mixture.
  5. Season and garnish: Add salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil.
  6. Toss and serve: Toss the salad to combine all ingredients well. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

This pasta salad is a vibrant and refreshing dish perfect for any occasion. It features a delightful mix of cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, black olives, and crumbled feta cheese. The ingredients come together in a harmonious blend of colors and textures, making it both visually appealing and delicious. The salad is tossed with a simple yet flavorful dressing made from olive oil and red wine vinegar, which enhances the natural flavors of the fresh vegetables.

Preparing this pasta salad is straightforward and quick. After cooking and cooling the pasta, you simply chop the vegetables and mix everything in a large bowl. The addition of fresh basil adds a fragrant and herbal note, while the feta cheese provides a tangy, creamy contrast. This versatile salad can be served immediately or chilled for a more refreshing taste, making it an ideal choice for picnics, barbecues, or as a light meal on a warm day.
